Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Recuro does not collect any personal information. All your tasks, projects, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Your tasks, completion history, notes, projects, and app preferences are stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's secure data storage.

Analytics

Recuro uses TelemetryDeck for privacy-friendly, anonymous analytics. We collect information about how you interact with the app — such as which screens you visit, which features you use, and what actions you take — in order to identify issues, fix bugs, and improve the overall experience.

We never collect the actual content you create — your task names, notes, project names, or any other personal data you enter into the app. We also do not collect device identifiers or any information that could be used to identify you personally. TelemetryDeck is GDPR compliant and does not use cookies or fingerprinting.

iCloud

If you use the iCloud backup feature, your data is stored in your personal iCloud Drive account. We do not have access to this data — it's fully controlled by your Apple ID.

No Third-Party Services

Recuro does not integrate with advertising networks, data brokers, or any services that would access your personal information.

App Store

Recuro is distributed through the App Store. Apple may collect certain information as described in Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to this information.

Children's Privacy

Recuro does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not collect any personal information from any users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at jhrad.apps@proton.me
